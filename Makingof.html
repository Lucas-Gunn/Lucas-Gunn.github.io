The Making of the Website

This page is just for me to write about the website and my experience making it. If you don’t care for that and just want space stuff, feel free to skip this page.

This website originated from a silly little thought in my head: “How do characters in movies always miraculously end up on some strange planet, isn’t space mostly nothing?”, that thought then spiraled into hours upon hours of research about our Universe, all for a school project about HTML.

One of the first major issues I encountered during development was with the page selection screen. I originally intended for the screen to replicate the Boötes constellation, but I completely forgot that the screen would stretch out. This is one of the issues that I just left as is, I think that keeping it wider allows it to fill out the screen nicely. Also I'm lazy.

Throughout development I would almost always be listening to music, which is why I ended up including Spotify Embeds on every page. It became a fun little side activity for me to find different songs that I believed fit the page, e.g. For Voids I put “Only You” for pretty obvious reasons. David Bowie’s music ended up being the main inspiration for the whole aesthetic of the website (hence “Space Oddity” being the first song.

Thanks for reading this :)
